1. Game Development Level 1 & Level 2 :


Grade 3 – Grade 5

Duration – 10 days each level

Instead of playing games, let your kids develop their own games

FAQs



1. Are there live online sessions daily as part of the course?


In our first batch of Online course, we conducted live online for the first few sessions . However, we faced issues with connectivity, one kid had bandwidth issues and could not hear us/ see our desktop or we could not see/ hear the kids and if one kid has these issues we will be wasting other kids time also. So, we decided to change our strategy. Now, we share recorded interactive videos and kids complete their programs/ assignments and submit them. If the kid has any questions or facing difficulty in any assignments, we have a one-on-one session with the kid on that day.

2. Why are your online courses not free? There are many who are providing for free..


Over the years, we have conducted a few free course. If it's a single session, then a free session works. However, for a 10 day course, we have
  1. Assignments.
  2. Daily deadline for assignments.
  3. Day to day Feedback on assignments.
  4. One on one Sessions(if required)
  5. Grading
When it is paid, the parents keep a track of their kid's progress and the kids learn more out of it. Due to the Corona situation, we have provided discounted price.

3. What if the kid has questions while he is going through the videos?


We are available to answer the questions through Google Classroom.

4. Can you give more details on the curriculum?


Game Development: A few schools teach Scratch programming to students. However, the depth of learning is very less. 3 years of school curriculum is equal to 2-3 days of our curriculum. We start teaching with the basics and move on to complex features that way the kids learn in a systematic approach.

Android App Development: Kids need not have prior knowledge on software development. They learn from the basics, develop their own apps and test them too. They will learn to code the different UI components and also learn to code the sensors.

5. Can you give us more details about RoboLectro STEAM Studio?


RoboLectro STEAM Studio (www.robolectro.com), Bangalore was founded by 2 Engineers who worked in the corporate world. They quit their corporate career to follow their passion in teaching kids the practical way of learning STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths). They are LEGO Certified Trainers from Carnegie Mellon University, PA, USA. They conduct different courses like LEGO Robotics, Coding, Electronics, Practical Science and Communication Skills. They also mentor kids for globally acclaimed robotics competitions like WRO, FLL, RoboCup etc. The teams they have mentored have represented India in International competitions. Please visit www.robolectro.com or https://www.facebook.com/pg/Robolectro for more details.

We conduct online LEGO Robotics classes for those kids who have LEGO Mindstorm EV3 31313 at home. Contact Us for more details.
