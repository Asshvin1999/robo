CSR Initiative

Home CSR Initiative

Mentoring for FLL 2020 competition:

As part of our Social Resonsibility Initiative, we mentored kids in Government school for FLL 2020 (Robotic kit and material required for competition sponsored by Caterpillar). The team secured the Overall Championship award and Best Robot Performance awards held by Caterpillar, Chennai. They qualified for Nationals and competed against 42 teams in Velammal International School, Chennai

LEGO Robotics workshop in Government

The Government School kids miss the exposure that the other kids get. To give back to society, we conduct LEGO robotics workshops in Government schools. The kids build robots and write the program. The kids love to see their creation working.

Teaching Practical Science in Government school:

As part of BEST initiative by Mr. Tejasvi Surya, we teach Practical Science for kids in Government schools.
