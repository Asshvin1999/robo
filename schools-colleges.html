Robotics Workshops In Engineering Colleges

Home Schools/Colleges
Are the engineering students learning the skills required for a company? Are the skills that they learn practical and hands-on approach?

We conduct courses like ‘Learn Design Engineering using LEGO Robots’, where the engineering students learn about the different steps of Design Engineering. We provide a problem statement, students brainstorm on a solution, design a prototype, build a robot, code it and test their solutions. Through this, they understand the process of design engineering through hands-on and practical approach.
