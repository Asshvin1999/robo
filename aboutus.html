Little About Us

We started RoboLectro STEAM Studio to follow our passion

Passionate

Two Engineers who worked in corporate world quit their jobs to follow their passion in teaching students. (A spark that was lit when it helped their son)

Latest Courses with STEM based curriculum

Kids learn STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths) through our practical, hands-on based courses. Concepts are explained by giving real world examples. Varied courses like LEGO Robotics, Electronics, Coding, Communication Skills for students of age group of 5 years -20 years.

LEGO certified trainers

Kids will be taught by trainers trained by LEGO certified trainers from Carnegie Mellon University, PA, USA

Outreach Programs

Frequent Interactive sessions with experts across different domains(IISc, ISRO and many more) helps the students in developing their innovation and discovery skills.

Mentoring for competitions

Kids who are passionate are mentored for globally acclaimed robotics and science competitions like FLL, WRO, Robocup, Google CodeToLearn, etc. Our teams have won more than 15 awards since the past 2 years

Represented India and won awards in Robotics Competition

Our students have represented India and won Best Technical Skills Award in Robocup Asia Pacific International competition held in Moscow, Russia (Nov 2019) and will also be representing in Japan (Oct 2020).

Welcome To RoboLectro STEM Studio


The RoboLectro STEM Studio is a place where kids explore Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Math through fun and by building hands-on activities. We offer After-school programs, Weekend workshops, Workshops in Apartments, Summer camps, Winter camps, Vacation camps, and STEM-based birthday parties and corporate events.
