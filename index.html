Welcome to Our School

Mission Vision Objective

At RoboLectro STEAM Studio, we move from idea generation to implementation, tapping into each childâ€™s natural desire to experiment and explore. Our focus is to offer the best possible learning through hands-on, project-based activities that grant students an opportunity to solve real-world problems through out-of-the-box thinking and develop the skills that are necessary to excel in the technologically-advanced world.

We started RoboLectro STEAM Studio to follow our passion. When we were in USA, we heard about the LEGO Mindstorm EV3 Robotics kit. Looking at the price of the kit, we were a little hesitant to buy it for our 7 year old son. Since it was very interesting, we bought it and introduced it to our kid. Within a couple of hours, he started to build the robot and was thoroughly enjoying it. Over the years, he has learnt the building and writing the code for the robots and competed in many robotics competitions like WRO, Robofest, etc. We being engineers and working in the corporate world, we realized how his skills of Design, Mechanical, Electronics, writing software, observation, curiosity and science concepts have grown and how it would be useful for his future. As a parent, if itâ€™s helped our kid, then we were certain that robotics and practical way of learning science would make a difference in the way kids learn science. We took the franchisee of Science Utsav to teach Practical Science and started RoboLectro STEAM Studio to teach STEM concepts through LEGO robotics.

There is a huge gap between how Science is taught in developed countries against Indian education. STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths) is never taught within the four walls. Our curriculum has been developed in such a way that the students learn STEM concepts through hands-on, practical and activity based. Learn STEM the right way:
Make an observation
Ask a question
Make a Hypothesis
Conduct experiments
Draw Conclusions
Report your results
